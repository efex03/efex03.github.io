Welcome to Industrial Chaos...

What the hell is this?

The goal of this game mode is to become powerful through collecting items and dominating territory with organization members. It's that simple... Kill or be killed that is the game. Below is an in-depth guide of how to become more powerful.

How do I play?

The Basics

When you first start, you will notice that you do not have any money or items. Luckily, it is pretty easy to get some. You'll probably notice there is trash all over the place.

You might also notice something on the HUD. Your character has a 'PVP Value' and there is some text on the bottom right corner. Players cannot just be killed if they are poor. This is to prevent powerful players from bullying new players or player trying to rebuild. Your PVP Value is simply the sum of the value of all of your items and money. If it exceeds $10,000, then your character can be killed away from the safebox!

Be aware that you are not invincible to do whatever. You can become vulnerable for any of the following reasons: you picked up an item, you used a weapon, you have your physgun or tool gun out or you have PVP time.

Getting Started

When you first start, you will notice that you do not have any money or items. Luckily, it is pretty easy to get some. You'll probably notice there is trash all over the place. Collect this trash by pressing E on it and putting it into your inventory. Then go find a trash can and press E on it to throw away your collected trash. You will be rewarded for cleaning up this shit hole of a city. Collect enough money to purchase a money printer ($500).

Now that you are collecting money, you don't want to loose it to some fucker who is just gonna shoot you for it. Go to a safebox and press E on it. Deposit your money using the menu and any items you got. It is probably not worth storing trash in your safebox. When you die, you won't loose any items/money stored in your safebox.

Making The Real Money

Now that you got some cash in your wallet, you can stop collecting trash like a poor bastard you are. Here is where you character will start making enough money to buy fun things like guns and armors. In order to print money, you will need to purchase property. Go up to your desired property and press F2 on it to buy it. Once you bought it, you can go inside and lock it using your keys. Note that you'll have to pay rent on the door every so often, so make sure you have enough money in your wallet (not safebox) or you will loose your property!

When you go inside your newly purchased property, you'll notice outlets on the walls. These will power your printers. You must own the property to use the outlets. You will see "0/2" on the outlet. This indicates that there are 0 out of the maximum 2 devices connected to the outlet.

Purchase a printer from the Business menu in the 'Manufacturing' tab. And power the printer using the power tool.

Organizations

The best way to make money is money printing and claiming territory. You can claim territory if you are in an organization. Territories glow purple and look like clock posts. Press E on them to claim them. Note that once you claim it, you have to stand near it for 2 minutes and will gain PVP time!
